About Me

Hey there! I'm Parth — an aspiring data analyst currently pursuing my MSc in Computer Science (Negotiated Learning) at University College Dublin (UCD), Ireland. I enjoy turning messy datasets into clear, actionable stories that actually make sense — and make an impact.

When I’m not deep in Excel sheets or dashboard design, you’ll probably find me watching (or yelling at) Formula 1, tennis, Counter-Strike tourneys, cycling events, cricket matches... you get the idea — sports basically own my weekends.

I’m also a full-on avgeek and machine nerd. If it’s made of metal and moves — planes, trains, bikes, cars — I’m into it. I could spend hours watching aircraft landings or getting lost in spec sheets for fun.

I’d describe myself as an ambivert: loud and expressive with the right people, quiet and observant when I need to recharge.

Walks + music = therapy. I don’t stick to one genre — if it sounds good, I’m vibing.

I’m also big on staying organized. Color-coded folders? Yes please. Whether it’s my work or my playlists, I like things structured.

When I’m not exploring a dataset, I’m probably binge-watching something intense — sci-fi, thrillers, crime dramas are my go-tos.

If you're curious, I even turned my watch history into a data project — yep, Content Analysis meets Netflix binging.

Always exploring, always analyzing — I’m excited about the stories hidden in data and how they can help people make smarter, faster decisions. Let’s build something cool with it.
